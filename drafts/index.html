Bio

Hi! My name is Charlotte Thomas, I’m a 22 y.o French Developer, System admin, and general IT Specialist. I have a good job and I like it so if you’re a recruiter you do not need to send me an email.

Same if you’re looking for a developer to commission please search someone else I’m not your gal.

I love a variety of language, my languages of choice nowadays are Rust and OCaml.

My favourite projects are Calc and BaguetteSharp.

I like writing parsers, interpreters, and compilers. I settled into a basic technique of pratt-parsing from Calc and newer projects.
